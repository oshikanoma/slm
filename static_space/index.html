The Verifier

A cited, evidence-bounded newsroom copy desk — it checks AP style and verifies claims against real sources, or says so plainly.

Check AP Style — live

Deterministic AP Stylebook checker (14 rule areas), running in your browser. Type a sentence; it flags every violation instantly. Try: "The board has 5 members and meets at 3:00 PM on December 8, said Smith."

Verify a claim — the model's job

The fine-tuned SLM verifies claims against retrieved sources — it only says supported when it can quote the verbatim backing sentence, and refuses to vouch otherwise. The model runs in the notebook / local app and is published on the HF Hub. Below is a real base-vs-tuned run on the same retrieved sources:

Claim: “The James Webb Space Telescope launched on December 25, 2021 from French Guiana.”
Retrieved: NPR, NASA, Wikipedia (via live web search)
